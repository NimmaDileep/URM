Our Services

At URM Application, we provide several services aimed at promoting diversity in academia.

Academia Services

For academic institutions, we offer a platform to advertise available positions and access a database of URM candidates.

URM Candidate Services

For URM candidates, we offer a database of available positions at various academic institutions, along with resources to support their applications and career development.

DEI Officer Services

For DEI officers, we offer resources and a platform for promoting diversity initiatives, as well as access to data on diversity in academia.

Recruiter Services

For recruiters, we offer access to a diverse pool of candidates, as well as tools to assist in the recruitment process.
