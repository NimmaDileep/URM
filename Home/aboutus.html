About URM Application

URM Application is dedicated to enhancing diversity in academia. Our aim is to connect underrepresented minority (URM) candidates with academic institutions, DEI officers, and recruiters.

We believe that everyone should have an equal opportunity to advance their education and career, and we're committed to making that happen through our platform.

We invite you to join us in our mission to create a more diverse and inclusive academic community.
